About

I'm a final-year B.Tech Information Technology student at Medi-Caps University, graduating in 2027. Most of my hands-on learning has come from building two full products end-to-end: Tradezione, a stock trading simulator with real portfolio math, and FinGoal, a suite of financial planning calculators people can actually use to think about SIPs, EMIs, and retirement.

I like the MERN stack because it gets out of the way — React and Tailwind for interfaces that feel responsive, Node and Express for the logic, MongoDB for the data. Lately I've also been building a security-aware base, earning Fortinet's cybersecurity certifications, and sharpening core CS fundamentals: data structures, DBMS, operating systems, and computer networks.
